    - `Plot raw fluorescences traces, neuropil-corrected traces, and dF/F <_static/examples/nb/cam.html#Fluorescence-Traces>`_
    - `Run neuropil correction <_static/examples/nb/cam.html#Neuropil-Correction>`_
